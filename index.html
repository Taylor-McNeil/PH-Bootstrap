    • About Us

    PantherHackers is a technology group founded in 2015, with the mission to build a bridge between higher education and post-secondary industries. "We believe in the hacker culture. We are individuals who enjoy the intellectual challenge of creatively overcoming and circumventing limitations to achieve clever and novel solutions" To achieve this we support our hackers by hosting weekly interest group meetings on a variety of topics, teaching workshops for in-demand skills, and hosting hackathons and ctfs to sharpen our skills against one another.
